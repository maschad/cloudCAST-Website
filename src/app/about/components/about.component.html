About Cloudcast

Mission and Examples

CloudCAST Mission

  1. Speech Professionals (Therapists, Teachers, Clinicians..) are often overworked.

  2. Clients don’t get the one-to-one time they need.

  3. There is developing technology which promises to help.

  4. We aim to give speech professionals the opportunity to use and improve the technology.

  5. The professionals must be in control of the technology.

  6. The technology must adapt to the professional’s needs.

  7. The data collected while the technology is in use will provide a basis for further research and development.

  8. CloudCAST will provide a resource which is free to the community.

  9. This resource will be available ‘in the cloud’, with computation done remotely.

CloudCAST Examples

  1. Therapy aids based on speech recognition.

  2. Speech disorder diagnostics.

  3. Literacy Tutors.

  4. Personalised recognisers for people with speech disorders.

  5. Voice command and control for people who can’t use keyboards.

  6. Communication aids for people whose speech is hard to understand.
